Arcanist Information

Melania

An over-achiever from London that plans and theorizes through logic. Accompanied by her handbag, Ms. Acey, she becomes a worldwide known criminal. Also known to be a perfectionist in her craft, questioning if she did the right action. Melania doesn't steal for the money, but mainly for the thrill and the test of the item's security system.


Specializes in the Beast element or "Afflatus." Deals mental damage to her enemies and best used as the main damage dealer within a team. Best accompanied by a support and a healer.



Fun Fact!

Melania devises three (3) different plans when you select three (3) of her skills during combat.

Medicine Pocket

A researcher who excels in Biology, Medicine Pocket. Mostly talking about dogs and puppies in their letters, Medicine Pocket comes from Utah in USA. Abandoning her mother like how she abandoned the dogs that she cared for, Medicine Pocket left for good and decided to take up Biology and later learn how to work while having fun.


Also specializing in the Beast element or "Afflatus," Medicine Pocket deals mental damage to their enemies. At the same time, Medicine Pocket also heals their party while they attack.



Fun Fact!

Medicine Pocket is nonbinary, therefore accepting any pronouns. Best to use they/them to make them feel comfortable.

Tooth Fairy

A tall lady who works in dentistry. She works for the Foundation, a place where they stay within a safe time frame and train soon-to-be arcanists. She hails from Great Britain, taking care of her teeth as much as she does for those teeth she takes care of.


Casts Star "Afflatus" moves while doing mental damage. A great support with her skills of healing, debuffing, and purifying.



Fun Fact!

She is known for eating little creatures that work like the real Tooth Fairy, the creatures collect teeth and fly away, but when they approach Tooth Fairy, it's best to accept fate.
